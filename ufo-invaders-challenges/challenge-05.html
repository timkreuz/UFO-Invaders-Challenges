UFO Invaders: The Beginnings of a Defense System.


Introduction:

Wow - Those abductions look really scary. Let's get started on the defense system! Starting Branch: cc04-abduction Solution Branch: cc05-defense This starter defense system won't actually do anything to protect anybody, including the cats and chickens. But it will fly above the surface of the earth and will serve as the foundation for all our other defense mechanisms. The amazing thing we get to work with here will be Keyboard Events Handlers! That's right. When we hit the right arrow key, the defense system will move to the right. Unless of course it reaches the edge of the screen in which case the defense system will do absolutely nothing. And similar logic will exist for the left arrow key.
